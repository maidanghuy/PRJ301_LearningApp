About us

G-Easy is a promising and growing rapidly educational technology startup in Vietnam. G-Easy is an English learning software from basic to advanced to help learners easily approach English. It currently has more than 13 million users in 101 countries around the world, and has offices in Portugal, Vietnam, India, Indonesia and Japan.
